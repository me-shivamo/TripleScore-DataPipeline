1<sup>st</sup> order kinetics

$$t = ?$$

74. Given :

$$\Delta H_{\text{sub}}^\circ [\text{C}(\text{graphite})] = 710 \text{ kJ mol}^{-1}$$

$$\Delta_{\text{C-H}} H^\circ = 414 \text{ kJ mol}^{-1}$$

$$\Delta_{\text{H-H}} H^\circ = 436 \text{ kJ mol}^{-1}$$

$$\Delta_{\text{C=C}} H^\circ = 611 \text{ kJ mol}^{-1}$$

The  $\Delta H_f^\circ$  for  $\text{CH}_2=\text{CH}_2$  is \_\_\_\_\_  $\text{kJ mol}^{-1}$  (nearest integer value)

Ans. (25)

![](https://tripple-score.sfo3.digitaloceanspaces.com/TripleScore/JEE_Mains_2025_Shift_1_Question_Paper_Apr_03_With_Solutions/5a515f5f65583e486406aff3dd71e876_img.jpg)

Sol.

$$\begin{array}{ccc} 2\text{C}_{(\text{s})} + 2\text{H}_{2(\text{g})} & \longrightarrow & \text{CH}_2 = \text{CH}_{2(\text{g})} : [\Delta H_f^\circ]_{\text{C}_2\text{H}_{4(\text{g})}} \\ \downarrow & \swarrow & \\ 2\text{C}_{(\text{g})} + 4\text{H}_{(\text{g})} & & \end{array}$$

$$[\Delta H_f^\circ]_{\text{C}_2\text{H}_{4(\text{g})}} = 2 \times [\Delta H_{\text{sub}}^\circ]_{\text{C}_{(\text{s})}} + 2 \times \Delta H_{\text{H-H}}^\circ - 1 \times \Delta H_{\text{C=C}}^\circ - 4 \times \Delta H_{\text{C-H}}^\circ$$

$$\Rightarrow [\Delta H_f^\circ]_{\text{C}_2\text{H}_{4(\text{g})}} = (2 \times 710) + (2 \times 436) - 611 - 4 \times 414$$

$$\Rightarrow [\Delta H_f^\circ]_{\text{C}_2\text{H}_{4(\text{g})}} = 25 \text{ kJ / mol}$$
